Plastic Anxiety

A research-led visual experiment on fast fashion, luxury branding, garment labels, washing tags, and the plastic residue hidden inside contemporary clothing systems.

92M tonnes textile waste annually
60% clothing materials made from plastic
85% textiles go to dumps each year
25% clothing waste incinerated
500K tons microplastics enter oceans annually
Data source: European Parliament; UNEP / Global Fashion Agenda
